INTRODUCTION

The International Automotive Business Convention & Exhibition 2026 (IABCE 2026) in Malaysia will be a landmark event bringing together automotive industry leaders, innovators, policymakers, and technology providers from around the world.

This event will serve as a pivotal platform for discussing the future of the automotive sector in the context of Malaysia's growing role as a key player in the Southeast Asian automotive market and the global automotive supply chain.

With Malaysia's strategic location, robust manufacturing capabilities, and increasing focus on electric vehicle (EV) adoption and sustainable mobility, IABCE 2026 will position the country at the forefront of automotive innovation in the region.

The event will feature cutting-edge product exhibitions, thought-provoking panel discussions, and high-level networking opportunities to drive growth and collaboration within the automotive industry.
