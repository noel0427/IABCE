International Automotive Business Convention & Exhibition 2026 (IABCE)

Malaysia | Organized by International Automotive Associations

About the Event

The International Automotive Business Convention & Exhibition 2026 (IABCE 2026) in Malaysia will be a landmark event bringing together automotive industry leaders, innovators, policymakers, and technology providers from around the world.

With Malaysia’s strategic location, robust manufacturing capabilities, and growing focus on electric vehicle (EV) adoption and sustainable mobility, IABCE 2026 will position the country at the forefront of automotive innovation in the region.

The event will feature cutting-edge product exhibitions, thought-provoking panel discussions, and high-level networking opportunities to drive growth and collaboration within the automotive industry.

Aims and Objectives

  • 1. Catalyse Industry Transformation: Support the transformation of the automotive industry in Southeast Asia by promoting innovation, sustainable practices, and new mobility solutions.
  • 2. Promote Malaysia as an Automotive Hub: Highlight Malaysia’s role as a regional leader in automotive manufacturing, EV production, and smart mobility.
  • 3. Foster Collaboration & Partnerships: Facilitate partnerships between global automotive players, tech companies, energy providers, and Malaysian institutions.
  • 4. Advance Sustainable Mobility Solutions: Address environmental challenges and promote adoption of green technologies, electric vehicles, and clean energy.
  • 5. Knowledge Sharing: Provide a platform for thought leadership, research dissemination, and the exchange of best practices.
  • 6. International Cooperation: Unite associations from Malaysia and partners internationally as one.
